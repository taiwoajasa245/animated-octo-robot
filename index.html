About Samfold Consults

Founded in 2015, Samfold Consults began as a small mentorship program for aspiring developers and has grown into a premier education hub for technology and finance professionals.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional education and industry requirements by providing practical, skills-based training that leads to tangible career outcomes.

We believe in learning by doing, which is why all our programs emphasize hands-on projects, real-world simulations, and direct mentorship from industry practitioners.
